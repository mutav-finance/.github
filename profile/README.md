# MUTAV Finance

 > REAL ASSET · REAL YIELD
 
 The on-chain rental guarantee fund. Opening Brazil's $1.1B rental guarantee market to crypto market for the first time.

  ## The market
  
  Brazil has 46.5M people paying rent. Behind every contract sits a guarantee instrument generating consistent
  fees, a $1.1B ecosystem that has grown 195% since 2020, concentrated in the hands of a few players with no 
  outside participation and no transparency. Until now.

  ## What we're building

  MUTAV is the infrastructure that opens this market onchain. Investors access the yield generated by real    
  rental guarantee contracts, participating directly in a market previously closed to outside capital, within
  a regulated legal framework.

## Protocol repos

The protocol delivery is two repos (decision: [`mutav-stellar#57`](https://github.com/mutav-finance/mutav-stellar/issues/57)):

| Repo | Scope |
|---|---|
| [`mutav-stellar`](https://github.com/mutav-finance/mutav-stellar) | `Fund` Soroban contract + TS SDK. Audited surface. Canonical architecture: [`docs/architecture/`](https://github.com/mutav-finance/mutav-stellar/tree/main/docs/architecture). |
| [`mutav-app`](https://github.com/mutav-finance/mutav-app) | Turborepo monorepo: persona apps (agency / fund / admin / …) + the **Mutav API** (Convex backend). |

Strategy / brand / whitepaper: [`mutav`](https://github.com/mutav-finance/mutav).
Standalone web3 portal [`mutav-fund`](https://github.com/mutav-finance/mutav-fund) is folding into `mutav-app/apps/fund/` and soft-deprecating ([`mutav-fund#11`](https://github.com/mutav-finance/mutav-fund/issues/11)).
