# MUTAV Finance

 > REAL ASSET · REAL YIELD
 
Turning Brazilian rental guarantees into a real-yield onchain asset. Real contracts. Real yield.

## The idea

 Brazil has 46.5 million people paying rent. Behind every rental contract sits a guarantee
 instrument and behind every guarantee sits concentrated capital, held by a handful of
 players with no outside participation and no transparency.
  
## What we're building

We are building the infrastructure to turn rental guarantees into a real-yield asset
accessible to anyone, operating on two fronts:

  **Rental Guarantee:** acts as a guarantee layer for real estate agencies. 
  **Tokenized RWA Fund:** crypto investors access the yield generated by real rental guarantee 
  contracts, participating directly in a billion-dollar market previously closed to outside capital
